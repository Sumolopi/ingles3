2. A handful of memories

Before preparing our travel to Vinci, let´s answers these questions:

Have you ever gone on a trip with your family or friends? Where did you go? Who did you go with? 

What did you do? Can you tell some anecdotes? Let’s share our best experiences! 

The things that you remember at this stage will help you with the final task.

Definition:

The itinerary from one place to another.

El itinerario de un lugar a otro.

Example:

The children enjoyed the trip.

Los niños y niñas disfrutaron del viaje.

Definition:

Use something together.

Usar algo juntos o juntas.

Example:

We share the same room.

Compartimos la misma habitación.

1. Group activity: Memories

Maybe you keep pictures, souvenirs or just good memories from one of your past trips. Bring one of them to class and talk to your classmates about it.

Definition:

The mental capacity of remembering something or someone.

La capacidad mental de recordar algo o a alguien.

Example:

He has good memories of his school trip.

Tiene buenos recuerdos de su viaje de estudios.

You can talk about...

- Destination.

- Travellers.

- Means of transportation.

- Activities.

- Anecdotes or stories.

- Budget or cost.

- Overall feeling.

Lumen dice...

Puedes hablar de:

  • Destinos.
  • Viajeros.
  • Medios de transporte.
  • Actividades y anécdotas.
  • Coste.
  • Cómo te sentiste en general.

Your travel memories or expectations

Remember your last trip or that day out in the city, in the country or at the beach. 

If you can’t think of anything or you can’t find any pictures, you can search the Internet for some pictures of a trip that represents yours or that you would like to do.

How did it feel?

How did you feel when you saw a beautiful view? 

How do you feel when you do a fun activity with friends?
