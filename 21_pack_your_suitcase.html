2.1. Pack your suitcase!

Before packing our suitcase, we should ask ourselves two questions. 

What kind of activities are we going to do at Vinci? 

What will we need to carry out these activities?

1. What to do

Our destination is Vinci, but, what do we know about this place?  It may be hot or cold there?  The following activities will help you think about different activities and the items you will need to carry them out.

Option A: Objects and activities

Investigate about the different activities and items you will need to enjoy Vinci as much as Leonardo. 

Option B: Choose your travel friend

1. Look at your travel friend 

Look at the pictures of Ana and Maria. They are leaving soon. What activity do you think they will do?

Ana

Possible answers:

Ana: She likes hiking. She enjoys playing the guitar. She likes spending time in nature. 

María: She loves travelling. She likes travelling by plane. Maybe she enjoys the city.

María

2. Listen to your travel friend

Listen to Ana and María who are getting ready for their next trip. Who do you think is Ana? And who is María?

Track 1     


Track 2   

Who do you want to go with? Why?

Which activities would you like to do with them?

Option C: Dare to record your experience

Think of your best travelling experience. What did you do? Explain why you liked it and record yourself.
