4.1. The travel plan

 

A travel plan is a really useful tool that will help you not to get lost during your travel moreover than having everything about the travel under control. 

1. My route

All the elements of a travel plan are included in the following interactive travel plan. Have a look at it:

A custom-made trip for you

#1

Set off with the most comfortable means of transport! This trip includes a five-star-night train for the outbound journey and a business class plane ticket for the inbound journey. During our stay in our destination a coach can be available for us 24/7.

#2

This country is unpredictable. It can be cold and it can rain heavily (and you will freeze); and all of the sudden it can be extremely hot (and you will be boiling hot). So pack your luggage carefully! Include summer and winter clothes. Remember the luggage conditions, too: we can only take a backpack and a small suitcase. They can weigh up to twenty kilos.

 

#3

This wonderful trip has two options for the accommodation: you can check into a youth hostel or you can stay with a host family, a perfect option if you want to practise your English abroad.

#4

Every day we will receive a packed lunch to spend the day outside. Breakfast will always be with your host family or in the youth hostel. Dinners are not included. If you want, you can try new flavours in the best restaurants in the city.

#5

#6

The currency is different in this country, so it is advisable to ask for some money at the bank and take pocket money or a credit card abroad.

End

Make it happen. Our guests are absolutely delighted with this vacation. And everything for only €350!

  - A luxurious train to go to the destination.

- Un tren lujoso para ir al destino.

 - A plane to go home.

- Un avión para volver a casa.

  - A bus for every day.

- Un autobús para todos los días.

2. El tiempo cambia de repente.

Echa ropa de abrigo y ropa de verano.

Llevas una mochila y una maleta pequeña.

Tu equipaje pesa 20 kilos máximo.

3. Tienes dos opciones de alojamiento:

  - You stay in a hostel.

- Te quedas en un albergue.

- Te quedas en una casa.

  4. You have breakfast at home or at the hotel.

4. Desayunas en casa o en el hotel.

  The family or the hotel gives you the lunch. You decide where to eat it.

La familia o el hotel te da el almuerzo.

Tú decides dónde comerlo.

  You dine for free.

Cenas gratis.

5. Día 1: Vemos la catedral y el museo de las ciencias.

  A tour guide shows us the city.

Un guía turístico nos enseña la ciudad.

 In the evening, we have two options:

Por la noche, dos opciones:

  - We go to an escape room.

- Vamos a una escape room.

  Day 2: We go onto a balloon.

Día 2: Montamos en globo.

Visitas el parque de atracciones.

Día 3: Vamos al bosque.

We go sunbathing.

Tomamos el sol.

9. El viaje cuesta 350 euros.

2. Group activity

a) Write a title for the slides two to seven in the presentation.

b) Create a mind map including the titles and some of the new words found in the paragraphs.

Shall we organise the trip to Vinci?

In this activity you will learn how to organise a trip to Vinci taking into account the following questions:

  • What will I carry in my luggage?
  • What activities will I do there?
  • What places will I visit?
  • What means of transport will I use?
  • How much money will I need?

Time to close your eyes and imagine you are going to Vinci to meet Leonardo there.

It’s time to close your eyes and think about the different questions you would ask Leonardo. You can ask him about his inventions, his works of art and philosophy. 

Write down every question you want.

Remember to close your eyes, take a deep breath and let your imagination fly!
