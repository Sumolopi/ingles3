4.2. A suitcase of words

In this section we are going to practice with the words that we have seen in the previous section. 

All of them are related to travel experiences. 

They may help you when planning your tour.

1. Don't go blank

 It is always good to know what to say when planning a trip. These vocabulary and exersices will help you.

Vocabulary Travel Activities

Write as many sentences as you can that include the vocabulary below to describe how you want to plan your travel.

Vocabulary-trip Activity

Translate the following vocabulary. Write sentences about your trip to Vinci.

The travel agent

Imagine you are a travel agent and you are talking to two customers who want to spend a weekend in Vinci. They need transport, accommodation and a couple of activities. Think of a good plan for them and record yourself. Use the images to help you and look for the words you need.
