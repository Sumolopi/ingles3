3. Welcome on board

In the previous section, you have talked about lots of activities you can do in different places.

What about your town? Does it have something in common with Vinci? What can you do there?

You will have the chance to welcome exchange students in your town and make a two-day plan for them. This experience will help you in the final task.

1. Group activity: Welcome

Your school has been given an amount of money -€100  per student- to do an exchange with another school. Fifteen students from Vinci, the place where Leonardo da Vinci was, are going to visit your place for 2 days. What are you going to plan for them? 

What are you going to advise them to pack in their suitcase?

In small groups, draw and write down what you are going to recommend during their stay. Make up a travel plan for the exchange students. When you finish, present your travel plan to your classmates. Vote for your favourite one.

Definition:

A quantity of something.

Una cantidad de algo.

Example:

Sport gives an enormous amount of pleasure to many people.

El deporte da una enorme cantidad de placer a mucha gente.

Definition:

Students who attend a school in another country.

Estudiantes que asisten a una escuela en otro país.

Example:

We hosted two exchange students last year.

El año pasado alojamos dos estudiantes de intercambio.

Definition:

A period of remaining in the same place.

Un periodo en el que se permanece en el mismo lugar.

Example:

They planned a short stay at a hotel to celebrate their birthday.

Ellos planificaron una estancia corta en un hotel para celebrar sus cumpleaños.

2. Identify what I have to do

You already know what your challenge is and the activity that will bring you closer to the goal. But to succeed in your journey, you will need some strategies that will help you in this and other similar tasks. You will discover them in a diary that we call your Learning Journal. 

This time we suggest you open it and complete STEP 1 of the Learning Journal before you start the activity you have just read about!

Click here to download your Learning Journal.

Remember: 

- Ask your teacher whether you will fill it in on paper or on the computer. 

- If you fill it in on the computer, don't forget to save it on your computer when you finish it! Cheer up, you'll do great!

Ya conoces qué reto te proponemos alcanzar y te acabamos de plantear una actividad que te acercará a la meta. Pero para tener éxito en tu camino, necesitarás algunas estrategias que te servirán para esta y otras tareas parecidas. Las irás descubriendo en un diario que llamamos tu Diario de aprendizaje. 3. Have I been able to do the activity?

You have already finished the activity "Welcome"! The beginnings may have caused fear and insecurity.

If you complete STEP 2 of the Learning Journal (Will I be able to do it?), you will see that those feelings are common when you start a task and reflecting on this will help you reduce your insecurities in the next activities. 

Remember:

 - Ask your teacher whether you will fill it in on paper or on the computer.

 - If you fill it in on the computer, don't forget to save it on your computer when you finish it!
