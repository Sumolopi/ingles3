4. Fasten your seatbelts!


You are leaving soon and to have a successful trip, we need a well-organised travel plan.

I have chosen the following contents to help you prepare your own travel plan, to present it in class and to persuade your fellow students of your destination.

1. What you need to know to prepare and present the best travel plan

Parts of a travel plan and vocabulary related to trips

What are we going to include in the travel plan? Learn words related to means of transport, accommodation, costs, possible activities and luggage.

Talking about travelling and using future, imperative, modal verbs and first conditional

Are you going to talk about the possibilities that your trip offers? Practice the use of modal verbs (can, may, might). Are you also going to include when the activities are going to take place? Learn how to use the future tenses and the first conditional to talk about travelling.

Persuasive language

How are you going to convince your classmates that your travel plan is the best? Maybe you need to know how to persuade, that is to say, how to use descriptive and positive adjectives, imperative, slogans, repeating words and emotive language.

A good presentation

How are you going to tell your classmates your ideas? Have a look at these tips to prepare a good design for the written travel plan and a good oral presentation.

Plan to feel good!

Take small steps to reach big goals. 

2. Let’s start working!

To reach a goal, you need to be a good strategist. That is to say, you need to know methods, techniques, or "tricks" to get to where you want to be sooner or easier. Now I'm going to teach you a strategy, take advantage of it to reach your challenge!

You will find this strategy very useful to organize yourself, as it will help you to focus and learn what you need to know to create your travel plan. If you need help, click here. 

I am sure you'll do great!

Para llegar a una meta, es conveniente que seas un buen o una buena estratega. Es decir, tener métodos, técnicas o “trucos” para llegar antes o de forma más fácil donde tú quieres. Ahora te voy a enseñar una estrategia. ¡Aprovéchala para alcanzar tu reto!

Esta estrategia te resultará muy útil para organizarte, ya que te ayudará a concentrarte y aprender lo que necesitas saber para crear tu plan de viaje. Si necesitas ayuda, haz clic aquí.
